Meteorological input file convension based on https://github.com/carriemorrill/lake-model

Column 1: Year
Column 2: Month
Column 3: Day
Column 4: Hour of day
Column 5: Air temperature (K)
Column 6: Relative humidity (%)
Column 7: Wind speed (m/s)
Column 8: Surface incident shortwave radiation (W/m2)
Column 9: Surface downward longwave radiation (W/m2)
Column 10: Surface pressure (mb)
Column 11: Precipitation (mm) - optional for lake energy balance modeling
